## Esther Plomp

Hi! I'm Esther. I work as [postdoc/research developer at the University of Aruba](https://repository.ua.aw/entities/person/ff081ce9-5113-41b5-a369-ced06c7fa3d9) and I'm the project lead of the [Dutch Caribbean Digital Competence Network](https://dcdc.network/). Before that I was a Data Steward at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands (amongst others, working on getting data about what data/code is shared at the Faculty of Applied Sciences thanks to an eScience Center Fellowshop!). And before that I did a [PhD in bioarchaeology](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3929551), studying the isotopic composition of human teeth.
