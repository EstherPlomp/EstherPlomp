## Esther Plomp

Hi! I'm Esther. I work as postdoc/research developer at the University of Aruba. Before that I was a [Data Steward](https://www.tudelft.nl/en/library/current-topics/research-data-management/r/support/data-stewardship/contact/esther-plomp/) at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands. And before that I did a [PhD in bioarchaeology](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3929551), studying the isotopic composition of human teeth. 


### 🔭 I’m currently working on:
- Trying to get some data on what data/code is shared at the Faculty of Applied Sciences, TU Delft, thanks to an eScience Center Fellowship.
- Contemplating the dynamics of volunteer/paid contributions in open science/source communities, particularly The Turing Way, thanks to a Software Sustainability Institute Fellowship.

### 🌱 I’m currently learning:

- Adapting to a small university setting!
